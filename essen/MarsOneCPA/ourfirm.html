Our Firm

Michael S. Bathla, CPA, EA, MBA

Michael Bathla, the current President of MarsOneCPA, Inc started its predecessor entity, East West Tax International, as a sole proprietor in May 1995 in Reno, Nevada. He incorporated his business as East West Services Inc in 1999. While pursuing his Bachelors’ Degree in Accounting from the University of Nevada Reno, from 1994 to 2004, Mr. Bathla build a successful accounting and taxation business focusing on accounting, payroll and tax services for convenience stores, gas stations, motels and other small businesses. Mr. Bathla completed his MBA in Accounting and Taxation from Colorado Technical University in September 2009. Mr. Bathla passed his CPA exam in August 2015 and has been licensed as CPA in state of NV since July 21, 2016.

Since inception of his business, Mr. Bathla has consulted with hundreds of individuals and business clients about various tax planning strategies, and has gained extensive experience in tax planning, preparation of business returns for Partnerships, Limited Liability Companies, S Corporations, C Corporations and Not for Profit Entities. During these years, he has successfully represented his clients and resolved most complicated tax matters with various taxing authorities.

Outside office, Mr. Bathla enjoys spending time with family. He enjoys reading Bible, motivational books and articles related to personal and business finance. Doing regular exercises, having long morning walks with his wife Sarah, son Alex, and daughter Ronika, listening to children stories and BBQs on weekends is his passion. The family loves tasty dishes and the abundance of international foods available in the Biggest Little City in the World! The Bathlas cherish reading together daily Bible Scriptures and attending maximum congregation spiritual meetings at the Kingdom Hall as the family feels spiritual strength and good ethical values are back bone of its identity.

Sarah Bathla, E.A.,BTech (Computer Science)

Sarah Bathla, the present Office Manager for MarsOneCPA Inc has been working as Chief Financial Officer for East West Services Inc, the predecessor of East West CPA Inc since July 2008. Sarah holds a B.Tech degree in Computer Science and is currently pursuing her MBA in Accounting and Taxation. A genius in computers, Sarah has extensive experience in customer relations, and client problem resolutions. A specialist in preparing and ensuring that all reports and return are filed in a timely manner, she has always exceeded client expectations in relation to accounting, payroll and tax services. Her clear understanding and communication of various business and tax strategies, and effective resolution of Sales Tax, Payroll Taxes and Income Tax matters have added much to the success of East West CPA Inc, and its predecessor the East West Services Inc.

A wonderful Artist by nature, Sarah is a mother of two beautiful children, son Alexander, and daughter Ronika. She enjoys cooking mouth-watering, delicious meals, spending time with her family and playing with children until they are in their dreamlands. She never forgets her regimen of working out daily and watching TV shows with the children. Extremely clean and fair in her dealings, Sarah is very caring and well organized in her personal life.
